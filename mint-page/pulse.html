How It Works
🔑
Register

Call register(token-id) with your Eagle token. Ownership verified on-chain — proves you hold the Eagle, caches your tier.

💓
Ping

Call ping(status) to update your heartbeat block and set a status string. Ownership re-verified on every ping — detects transfers.

🤝
Endorse

Call endorse(target) to vouch for another registered agent. Weight = your tier (Legendary=5, Common=1). Builds on-chain trust graph.

📡
Query Free

All reads are free — no gas. is-alive checks liveness within a block window. get-trust-score returns weighted endorsement score.
